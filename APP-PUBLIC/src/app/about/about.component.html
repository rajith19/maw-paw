Our History

Founded in 2016 we might be a fairly new company to the industry, but we evolved into the best pet specialty store in Canada. Started in Cambridge Ontario, we now have stores all over Canada! We are proudly Canadian because we are using all Canadian products to boost the Canadian economy.

Our Culture

At Maw and Paw our Carry Out Culture is a way of life. All our staff are engaged and enthusiastic about what they do, and love to go the extra mile for our customers. Whether it is hosting a rescue in one of our stores, creating referral programs with professionals, running donation campaigns, or attending pet expos, our staff are committed to bettering our communities.

Our Vision

We have a saying here at Maw and Paw, that we are passionate about pets and the people they own. We believe that our furry companions deserve the best care and highest quality of products. We are partners, not competitors, with local groomers, humane societies, and other pet professionals. Our passion for pets and their well-being has led to adoption and pet license programs all over the Country.
